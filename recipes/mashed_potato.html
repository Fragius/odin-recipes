Mashed Potato

Home

Description

This is a recipe for mashed potatoes.

Each serving will be enough for one person, feel free to multiply ingredients by the amount of people you're making this for.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • a twenty fifth of the weight of the water in salt
  • two medium sized potatoes

How to make

  1. Fill 80% of the water's container in water.
  2. Heat the water until you see steam.
  3. While the water is heating remove the potatoes' shells
  4. Put the salt in the water (Add more salt if you must).
  5. Put the potatoes in the water for 25 minutes.
  6. Take the potatoes out of the water
  7. Mash the potatoes with a rock or something
