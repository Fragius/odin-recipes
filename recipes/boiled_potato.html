Boiled Potato

Home

Description

This is a recipe for boiled potatoes.

Each serving will be enough for one person, feel free to multiply ingredients by the amount of people you're making this for.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • A tweeny fifth of the weight of the water in salt
  • Two medium sized potatoes

How to make

  1. Fill 80% of the water's container in water
  2. Heat the water until you see steam
  3. Put the salt in the water
  4. Put the potatoes in the water for 25 minutes
  5. Take the potatoes out of the water
